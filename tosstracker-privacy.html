TossTracker  ·  Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

Overview

TossTracker is a cornhole scoring app developed by Sarah Clark. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how TossTracker handles your information.

Data Collection

TossTracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and does not connect to the internet.

Data Stored on Your Device

TossTracker stores the following information locally on your device to provide its core functionality:

  • Team names you enter for scoring games
  • Game scores and round history for completed and saved games
  • App preferences such as onboarding status and bag color selections

All data is stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

TossTracker does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

TossTracker does not share any data with third parties. No data is transmitted from your device.

Data Retention

All app data remains on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling TossTracker removes all associated data from your device.

Children's Privacy

TossTracker does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this document.
